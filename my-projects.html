    1.  Automation of Vertical Blanking Interval

    The project was my six month internship at Broad India Pvt Ltd at Bangalore. The Project was complete under the mentorship of Mr. Gurunath Kulkarni and under the supervision of Mr. Ravi Bhaskaran. you can read more about  Vertical Blanking Interval here. The project involved hours of geeky experimentation with Gage Card and Oscilloscopes. Once the signals were digitally captured it was stored in MATLAB . I made an algorithm to recognize the specific vsync and Hsyncs and then wrote separate algorithms for each kind of VBI. I cant share all the codes because of Broadcom's propriety guidelines. Still, I had prepared a report to beevaluated and  submitted in my university. I have described  VBI SIgnals, Gage Capture Card , My first line detection algorithm and the code for GUI in PERL. You can download the report here 2007A8PS029_Sharva Kant.doc (565798

     

    2. Intelligent Cardiac Monitoring System

    The project aims to develop a cheap and portable device for heart patients that keeps track of heart rhythm. In case of abnormality, the device will store the data for future diagnosis and also alert the patient or nearby persons such that the patient can call for help. Click on the hyperlink for the paper in the   ; online journal IJOE .You can read the full text by downloading our paper here   1541-6046-1-PB.pdf (820247)

     

    3. Reverse Solar Refrigeration

     It has been developed with the objective to help deliver vaccines and refrigeration to regions of the world without electricity or with inadequate electrical supply. A combined cycle of heating and cooling adsorption operating working has been proposed working directly on solar panels, hence, bypassing the use of conventional lead batteries and kerosene refrigerators.The project won second prize in the BITS Pilani Technical Meet-  APOGEE 2008. It then went on to be selected in the first round of KVPY(Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana) in the Stream SB( for undergraduates) category. You can read the full report here  kvpy report1.doc (823808). Also in the paper form  solar hybrid.doc (1237504
